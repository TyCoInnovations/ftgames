🏓 RETRO PONG 🏓

🎮 CONTROLS

⬆️ Up Arrow — Move Paddle Up
⬇️ Down Arrow — Move Paddle Down

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Keep the ball in play and score points by getting it past your opponent's paddle. The first player to reach the winning score is victorious!

⚡ HOW TO PLAY

  • 🏓 Bounce the ball back with your paddle.
  • 🎯 Make your opponent miss to score a point.
  • ⚡ The ball speeds up during longer rallies.
  • 🛡️ Stay focused and don't let the ball get past you.
  • 🏆 Reach the target score before your opponent.

💡 PRO TIPS

  • 🎯 Hit the ball near the edge of your paddle to change its angle.
  • ⚡ Stay near the center until you know where the ball is heading.
  • 👀 Watch the ball, not your paddle.
  • 🧠 Predict your opponent's next move.
  • 🏓 Consistency wins matches!
