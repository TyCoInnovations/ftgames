🛩️ PACIFIC DOGFIGHT 🛩️

🎮 CONTROLS

🖱️ Move the Mouse — Fly your fighter aircraft.
🔥 Left Click — Fire your machine guns.

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Pilot your WWII fighter through intense Pacific battles. Shoot down enemy aircraft, avoid incoming fire, and survive each mission to become a legendary ace!

⚡ HOW TO PLAY

  • 🛩️ Follow your mouse to steer your aircraft.
  • 🎯 Click to fire at enemy planes.
  • 💥 Destroy enemy fighters before they shoot you down.
  • ⚠️ Dodge bullets and avoid crashing.
  • 🏆 Survive each wave to achieve a higher score.

💡 PRO TIPS

  • ✈️ Keep moving to avoid enemy fire.
  • 🎯 Aim for targets with a larger threat.
  • ⚡ Short bursts improve your accuracy.
  • 👀 Watch the entire screen for incoming enemies.
  • 🦅 Stay calm under pressure—good pilots don't panic!
