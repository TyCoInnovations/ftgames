🛸 AMONG US PLATFORMER 🛸

🎮 CONTROLS

  • ⬅️➡️ Arrow Keys — Move
  • ⬆️ Up Arrow — Jump
  • 🚩 Green Flag — Restart the game

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Navigate through each platforming section, avoid hazards and enemies, and make it to the end of the level.

⚡ HOW TO PLAY

  • 🦘 Time your jumps carefully over gaps and obstacles.
  • 🚫 Avoid dangerous surfaces and enemies.
  • 🧭 Look ahead before committing to long jumps.
  • 🏆 Reach the end goal to clear the stage.

💡 PRO TIPS

  • 🎯 Use short taps for precise movement.
  • 🛑 Pause briefly before difficult jumps.
  • 🔁 If you miss a jump, restart quickly with the green flag.
  • 👀 Watch hazard patterns before rushing forward.
🛸 JUMP • DODGE • SURVIVE • REACH THE END 🏆
