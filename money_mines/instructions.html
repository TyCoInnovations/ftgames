💰 MONEY MINES 💣

🎮 HOW TO PLAY

💵 Enter your bet amount.
💣 Choose how many mines you want on the board.
▶️ Press BET to start the round.

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Click tiles to reveal money while avoiding the hidden mines. Every safe tile increases your multiplier and your potential winnings.

⚡ GAMEPLAY

  • 💚 Safe tiles increase your multiplier.
  • 📈 The more safe tiles you reveal, the more your cash-out grows.
  • 💣 Hitting a mine loses the entire bet.
  • 💸 Cash out before you get too greedy!
  • 🎲 More mines = higher risk and bigger rewards.

💡 PRO TIPS

  • 🧠 Don't get greedy—sometimes it's best to cash out early.
  • 💣 Fewer mines are safer but pay less.
  • 🚀 More mines give huge multipliers but are much riskier.
  • 💵 Bet only what you're willing to lose.
  • 🍀 Every round is random—good luck!

🔄 RESTART

😂 If your financial situation gets out of control, press the Green Flag to restart.
