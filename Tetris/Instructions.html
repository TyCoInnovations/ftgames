TETRIS

Stack falling blocks and clear lines to earn points!
Arrange the different block shapes to create complete horizontal rows.
When a row is completely filled, it disappears and you score points.
Don't let the blocks reach the top of the board, or it's Game Over!

Controls

← Left Arrow = Move Left
→ Right Arrow = Move Right
↑ Up Arrow = Rotate Block
↓ Down Arrow = Soft Drop (Faster Fall)
Space = Hard Drop (Instantly Place Block)

How to Play

1. Start the game and watch for the falling block.
2. Move and rotate pieces to fit them together.
3. Complete horizontal lines to clear them.
4. Clear multiple lines at once for a higher score.
5. Keep the board from filling up.
6. Survive as long as possible and beat your high score!

Tips

• Keep the stack low.
• Leave room for long pieces.
• Plan ahead before placing blocks.
• Clearing multiple lines at once gives more points.
• Stay calm as the game speeds up!

Good luck, and have fun playing Tetris!
