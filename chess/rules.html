♟️ HOW TO PLAY CHESS ♟️

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Checkmate your opponent's king.



♟️ PIECES

♔ King – Moves 1 square in any direction.

♕ Queen – Moves any number of squares horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

♖ Rook – Moves any number of squares horizontally or vertically.

♗ Bishop – Moves any number of squares diagonally.

♘ Knight – Moves in an L-shape (2 squares one direction, then 1 perpendicular). Can jump over pieces.

♙ Pawn – Moves forward 1 square (or 2 on its first move). Captures diagonally forward 1 square.


⚠️ SPECIAL RULES

👑 Check
Your king is under attack and must be protected.

💀 Checkmate
Your king is under attack and has no legal escape. You lose.

🤝 Stalemate
You are not in check but have no legal moves. The game is a draw.

🏰 Castling
Move the king and rook together.
Requirements:
• King has not moved.
• Rook has not moved.
• No pieces between them.
• King is not in check.
• King does not pass through check.

⚡ En Passant
A pawn may capture an opposing pawn that has just moved two squares as if it had moved only one.

👑 Pawn Promotion
When a pawn reaches the final rank, it becomes a Queen, Rook, Bishop, or Knight.


🤝 DRAW CONDITIONS

• Stalemate
• Threefold repetition
• 50-move rule
• Insufficient material
• Agreement between players


⏱️ ALWAYS THINK FOR 5 SECONDS BEFORE YOU MOVE!



🏆 PRO TIPS

🎯 Control the center.
🚀 Develop your pieces early.
🏰 Castle your king for safety.
🛡️ Protect your pieces.
👀 Always look for checks, captures, and threats.
