💣 MINESWEEPER 💣

🎮 CONTROLS

🖱️ Left Click — Reveal a tile
🚩 Right Click — Place or remove a flag
😊 Smiley Face — Start a new game



⚡ HOW TO PLAY

💣 Avoid every hidden mine.
🔢 Numbers tell you how many mines touch that square.
🧠 Use logic to work out where the mines are.
🚩 Flag dangerous tiles to stay organized.
🏆 Reveal every safe tile to win!


⭐ PRO TIPS

🎯 Your first click is always safe.
🔍 Compare multiple numbers before making a move.
🚩 Flags help you remember mine locations.
⚡ Think before you click—one mistake ends the game!
