🏀 FLAPPY DUNK 🏀

🎮 CONTROLS

🖱️ Click or press Space to flap the basketball.

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Guide the basketball through every hoop without touching the ground or missing a basket. Score as many points as possible!

⚡ HOW TO PLAY

  • 🏀 Tap to keep the ball in the air.
  • 🪙 Fly cleanly through each hoop to score.
  • ⚠️ Missing a hoop ends your run.
  • 🎯 Time each flap carefully to keep control.
  • 🏆 Beat your highest score!

💡 PRO TIPS

  • 🎯 Small taps give you better control.
  • 👀 Aim for the middle of each hoop.
  • ⚡ Don't spam clicks—stay smooth and steady.
  • 📈 Find a rhythm and stick to it.
  • 🏀 Stay calm as the pace increases.
🏀 KEEP FLAPPING • SWISH EVERY HOOP • SET A NEW HIGH SCORE 🏆
