🧩 TETRIS 🧩

🎮 CONTROLS

  • ⬅️ Left Arrow — Move Left
  • ➡️ Right Arrow — Move Right
  • ⬇️ Down Arrow — Soft Drop
  • ⬆️ Up Arrow — Rotate Piece
  • ⏬ Space — Hard Drop

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Arrange the falling blocks to complete full horizontal lines. Completed lines disappear, giving you more room to keep playing and earn a higher score!

⚡ HOW TO PLAY

  • 🧩 Move and rotate each tetromino as it falls.
  • 📏 Complete a full row to clear it.
  • ⚡ Clear multiple lines at once for bigger scores.
  • 📈 The game speeds up as you progress.
  • ☠️ The game ends when the blocks reach the top.

💡 PRO TIPS

  • 🧱 Keep your stack as flat as possible.
  • 🕳️ Avoid leaving holes beneath blocks.
  • 🏆 Save the long "I" piece for clearing four lines at once.
  • 👀 Think a few pieces ahead before placing.
  • ⚡ Stay calm—the faster the game gets, the more planning matters!
🧩 ROTATE • CLEAR LINES • SURVIVE • CHASE A HIGH SCORE 🏆
