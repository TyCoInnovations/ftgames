About us

We're 4 high school students who made an unblocked games website, because we wanted to make gaming easy and accessible for students.
With some help from TurboWarp, GitHub, and Homebase, we made games that are extremely hard to block and detect, with local static game rendering we make games that are both fun to play, and accessible from (almost) anywhere
We're FT Games, and we give you an ever-expanding library of great games that you can use from anywhere. All in one place.

Meet the developers


TyCoInnovations

TyCoInnovations is the founder and leader of FTGames, responsible for shaping the project’s direction and overall design. He started the site and has contributed much of the core game code used across the platform. With skills in HTML and some CSS, he focuses on building and organising the structure of the site. Creative and funny, he balances technical work with ideas that keep FTGames moving forward.



blabby-axolotl

blabby-axolotl is a key member of the FTGames team, working closely on ideas, design concepts, and supporting the overall direction of the project. He helps shape features and contributes to improving how games and pages are planned and structured. Known for being creative and collaborative, he brings fresh ideas to the team and helps turn rough concepts into something usable. While not always focused on coding, his input plays an important role in keeping FTGames balanced, fun, and constantly evolving.
