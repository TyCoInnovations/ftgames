About us

We're 3 year 7 students who made an unblocked games website, because we wanted to make gaming easy and accessible for students.
With some help from TurboWarp, GitHub, and Homebase, we made games that are extremely hard to block and detect, with local static game rendering we make games that are both fun to play, and accessible from (almost) anywhere
We're FT Games, and we give you an ever-expanding library of great games that you can use from anywhere. All in one place.

To learn more about our project, and/or contribute to it, check out our GitHub repository for FT Games!
