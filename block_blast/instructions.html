🧩 BLOCK BLAST 🧩

🎯 Objective

Place blocks on the board to complete full rows or columns. Completed lines disappear and earn you points. Keep making space and survive as long as possible to achieve the highest score!

🎮 How to Play

  1. 🧩 Drag one of the three available blocks onto the board.
  2. 🔄 Blocks cannot be rotated.
  3. ✨ Fill an entire row or column to clear it.
  4. 🎲 Once all three blocks are placed, three new ones appear.
  5. 💀 The game ends when none of the remaining blocks can fit.

🏆 Scoring

  • ⭐ Clear rows and columns to earn points.
  • 💥 Clear multiple lines at once for huge bonuses.
  • 🔥 Chain consecutive clears to build combos.

💡 Pro Tips

  • 🎯 Keep the centre of the board open.
  • 🧩 Save large spaces for awkward pieces.
  • 👀 Think several moves ahead.
  • 📦 Don't block yourself by filling corners too early.
  • ⚡ Look for opportunities to clear multiple lines together.
💎 PRO TIP: Check all three available pieces before placing one. The best move now isn't always the best move later!
