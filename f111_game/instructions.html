✈️ F111 ✈️

🎮 CONTROLS

  • ⬆️⬇️ Arrow Keys — Fly your aircraft.
  • ⚡ W — Increase the enemy fire rate (higher difficulty).
  • 🛡️ S — Decrease the enemy fire rate (lower difficulty).
  • 🚩 Green Flag — Restart the game.

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Control the aircraft on the left and dodge the incoming enemy bullets for as long as possible. The longer you survive, the greater the challenge!

💡 PRO TIPS

  • ✈️ Keep moving to avoid getting trapped.
  • 👀 Watch enemy bullet patterns carefully.
  • ⚖️ Adjust the difficulty using W and S.
  • 🎯 Small movements are often safer than large ones.
