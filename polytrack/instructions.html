🏎️ POLYTRACK 🏎️

🎮 CONTROLS

  • ⬆️ W / Up Arrow — Accelerate
  • ⬇️ S / Down Arrow — Brake / Reverse
  • ⬅️ A / Left Arrow — Steer Left
  • ➡️ D / Right Arrow — Steer Right
  • 🔄 R — Restart Track
  • 📍 Space — Respawn at Checkpoint
  • 📷 C — Change Camera View

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Race through every checkpoint and cross the finish line as fast as possible. Master each track and beat your personal best!

⚡ HOW TO PLAY

  • 🏁 Drive through every checkpoint in order.
  • 🚀 Keep your speed through jumps, loops and wall rides.
  • 💥 Recover quickly after crashes using Space or restart instantly with R.
  • ⏱️ Every second counts—race for the fastest time.
  • 🏆 Challenge yourself to climb the leaderboard.

💡 PRO TIPS

  • 🏎️ Smooth steering is usually faster than sharp turns.
  • ⚡ Carry as much speed as possible through corners.
  • 🛣️ Look for shortcuts and faster racing lines.
  • 🔁 Practice difficult sections until they're second nature.
  • 👻 Use your ghost replay to spot where you can improve.
