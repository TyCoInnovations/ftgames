🐍 SNAKE.FT 🐍

🎮 CONTROLS

  • 🖱️ Move Mouse / Cursor — Control the snake
  • ⚡ Hold Space — Speed Boost (Costs Length)

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Collect glowing orbs, grow your snake, and survive for as long as possible. Manage your length wisely and chase a new high score!

⚡ HOW TO PLAY

  • 🟢 Collect Orbs to grow longer and earn points.
  • ⚡ Pick up Lightning Bolts for a temporary speed boost.
  • 🚀 Hold Space to boost whenever you like.
  • 📉 Boosting with Space slowly shrinks your snake.
  • ☠️ If your snake becomes too short, it's game over.
  • 🧱 Avoid crashing into walls or yourself.
  • 🏆 Beat your highest score in this single-player challenge!

✨ ITEMS

  • 🟢 Orb — Grow longer and earn points.
  • ⚡ Lightning Bolt — Temporary speed boost.

💡 PRO TIPS

  • ⚡ Save your boost for emergencies or long journeys.
  • 📏 Don't waste too much length by boosting constantly.
  • 🎯 Plan your path before grabbing distant orbs.
  • 👀 Keep an eye on your tail as you grow.
  • 🏆 The best players balance speed with survival.
