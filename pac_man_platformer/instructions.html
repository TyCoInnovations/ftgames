🟡 PAC-MAN PLATFORMER 🟡

🎮 CONTROLS

⬅️➡️ Arrow Keys — Move
⬆️ Up Arrow — Jump

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Guide Pac-Man through each platforming level, collect pellets, avoid ghosts and hazards, then reach the 🍒 fruit to complete the level!

⚡ HOW TO PLAY

  • 🟡 Collect pellets to boost your score.
  • 👻 Avoid ghosts and dangerous obstacles.
  • 🦘 Jump across platforms and gaps.
  • 🍒 Reach the fruit to finish the level.
  • 🏆 Complete every level to beat the game!

💡 PRO TIPS

  • 🎯 Time your jumps carefully.
  • 👀 Watch ghost movement before rushing in.
  • 🟡 Grab every pellet if you're chasing a high score.
  • ⚡ Stay calm—precision beats speed.
  • 🍒 The fruit is your goal, so keep your eyes on it!
