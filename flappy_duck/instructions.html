🦆 FLAPPY DUCK 🦆

🎮 CONTROLS

🖱️ Click or press Space to flap your duck.

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Guide your green duck through the gaps between the pipes without crashing. Stay in the air for as long as possible and set a new high score!

⚡ HOW TO PLAY

  • 🦆 Tap to flap upward.
  • ⬇️ Gravity constantly pulls you down.
  • 🟢 Fly through every gap in the pipes.
  • 💥 Hitting a pipe or the ground ends the game.
  • 🏆 Keep going to beat your best score.

💡 PRO TIPS

  • 🎯 Use gentle taps instead of spamming.
  • 👀 Look ahead to line up the next gap.
  • ⚡ Find a steady rhythm.
  • 🦆 Stay near the middle of the screen when possible.
  • 🏅 Practice makes perfect!
