⛏️ PAPER MINECRAFT ⛏️

🎮 CONTROLS

⬅️➡️ A / D — Move
⬆️ W — Jump / Swim
⬇️ S — Sneak / Use
🖱️ Click — Mine / Attack / Place Blocks
🎒 E — Open Inventory
🔢 1-9 — Select Hotbar Slot



⚡ HOW TO PLAY

🌳 Gather wood and basic resources.
⛏️ Craft tools to mine stronger materials.
🏠 Build a shelter before night falls.
👾 Fight mobs and explore caves.
💎 Discover rare ores and survive as long as possible.



⭐ PRO TIPS

🌲 Collect plenty of wood on your first day.
🏠 Build a shelter before the monsters appear.
💎 Save valuable resources for better tools.
🔥 Torches are essential for exploring caves safely.



⛏️ MINE • CRAFT • BUILD • SURVIVE
