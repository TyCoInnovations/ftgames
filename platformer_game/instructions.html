Instructions for Platformer Game on FT Games Use the Arrow Keys to control the player.
Avoid the lava.
Go through the portal to advance to the next level.
Click the Green Flag to restart the game.
Press 1 for gambling.
Press R to restart the current level.
Press 3 for a disco effect. Warning: This will make the game unplayable, and you will need to press the Green Flag to restart the game.
