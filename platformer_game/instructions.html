🟩 PLATFORMER 🟩

🎮 CONTROLS

  • ⬅️➡️ Arrow Keys — Move
  • ⬆️ Up Arrow — Jump
  • 🔄 R — Restart the current level
  • 🎰 1 — Gambling Mode 😉
  • 🪩 3 — Disco Mode 🌈
  • 🚩 Green Flag — Restart the game

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Jump across platforms, avoid deadly lava, and make your way to the portal to complete each level!

⚡ HOW TO PLAY

  • 🦘 Jump over gaps and obstacles.
  • 🔥 Don't touch the lava—it'll send you back!
  • 🌀 Reach the portal to unlock the next level.
  • 🏆 Beat every level to complete the game.
  • 💪 Some levels are much harder than others, so don't give up!

🎉 SECRET FEATURES

  • 🎰 Press 1 for Gambling Mode.
  • 🪩 Press 3 for Disco Mode.
  • ⚠️ Warning: Disco Mode makes the game nearly impossible to play! Press the Green Flag to turn it off.
